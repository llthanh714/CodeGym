Balayage is a French word that means to sweep. It is a hair colouring technique where the Stylist will paint the hair to give golden reflects that the Sun naturally gives the hair in Summer. It is one of the most popular colour services requested in salons today and its popularity comes from the fact that it is extremely low maintenance and will grow out naturally.

Pre Booking Online

Hair is lightened in V shapes, away from the root to provide natural looking regrowth as the colour grows out over time. The lightened pieces are seamlessly blended throughout the hair, with the lightest pieces throughout the mid-lengths and ends.

Affordable Cost

The technique uses contrasting toning shades to help neutralise any brassiness post lightening. This helps provide an ultra-natural, soft and subtle result as well as adding gloss and shine to the hair.
